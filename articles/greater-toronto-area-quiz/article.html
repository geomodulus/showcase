How many municipalities in the Greater Toronto Area can you name?

Torontoverse Staff 2022-06-07

You’ve come to a site called Torontoverse, so at the very least you don’t have an aversion to the place — or you came here by mistake. Whatever.

Anyway, now that you’re here, let’s ask a simple question: How well do you know the place you may or may not have an aversion to?

More to the point: The Greater Toronto Area is made up of 25 distinct places — a mix of urban, suburban, and rural municipalities. How many of them can you name?

Take the Torontoverse quiz to find out!

Instructions:

  • Type the names of each municipality you can remember one by one into the empty field below.
  • When you type in a correct one (“Toronto” is kind of a gimme …), that municipality will be highlighted on the map.
  • You will have three minutes to guess them all.

And that’s it. When you’re ready, just hit the start button.

***QUIZ EXECUTION***

Scoring:

  • 21–25 correct: You’re the Greater-est. Drake should write a song about you.
  • 11–20 correct: Well played. You get the GTA, but not, like, all of it.
  • 1–10 correct: How did you get to this site, anyway? You technically don’t even know the GTA well enough to hate it.
  • 0 correct: You didn’t even get “Toronto”?!
